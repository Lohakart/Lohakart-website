Sustainable Metal Recycling Solutions

LohaKart is revolutionizing metal recycling with innovative processes and technology, creating a circular economy for metals while reducing environmental impact.

Our Recycling Process

At LohaKart, we employ an efficient, environmentally responsible process to recycle metals and minimize waste. Our comprehensive approach ensures maximum recovery and reuse of valuable materials.

Collection & Sorting

We collect scrap metal from various industrial sources and sort it based on metal type using advanced identification technologies.

Processing & Cleaning

The sorted metals undergo cleaning processes to remove contaminants, paints, and coatings, preparing them for further processing.

Shredding & Compacting

Materials are shredded into smaller pieces and compacted to reduce volume, making them easier to melt down in furnaces.

Melting & Purification

The processed scrap is melted in furnaces and further purified to remove impurities, resulting in high-quality recycled metal.

Solidification & Manufacturing

Molten metal is solidified into ingots, billets, or other forms ready for manufacturing into new products, completing the recycling loop.

Quality Assurance

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets industry standards and specifications for strength, purity, and performance.
